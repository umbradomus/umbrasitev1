What Umbra Does

We believe every house should provide more than just shelter.

We source undervalued properties, renovate them with care, and add regenerative features that deliver real savings and lasting resilience.

Our hands-on stewardship goes further: we teach homeowners how to care for and upgrade their properties themselves. Renters can choose from upgrades that immediately help lower their bills.

Homes that don’t just house families — they build dignity, equity, and a stronger future.
