What Umbra Does

We believe every house should provide more than just four walls and a roof.

We source undervalued properties, renovate them with care, and thoughtfully add regenerative features that deliver real savings and long-term resilience.

What truly sets us apart is our stewardship. We don’t just hand you the keys — we teach homeowners how to care for and upgrade their properties themselves. Renters get to choose from a menu of upgrades that can immediately lower their utility bills.

Homes that don’t just shelter families — they build dignity, equity, and a better future.
